Greetings from VinUniversity!
VinUniversity (VinUni) is the first private, not-for-profit Vietnamese university established by Vingroup, the largest
private conglomerate in Vietnam.
The University comprises of the College of Arts and Sciences; College of Business and Management; College of
Engineering and Computer Science; and College of Health Sciences and several Research Centers. We offer a fully
English-speaking academic environment. Every aspect of the University: its curricula, research, faculty, student body,
facilities, and campus life, is being developed to meet the highest standards set by the world’s leading accrediting and
ranking organizations, such as ABET, AACSB, WFME, ACEN, Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) and Times Higher Education
(THE).
VinUni’s founding benefactor - Vingroup is one of the biggest private conglomerates in Asia with the largest market
capitalization value in Vietnam. As a multisector corporation, Vingroup focuses on six main areas: Industrials &
Technology; Real Estate & Services; Infrastructure; Green Energy; Social Enterprises and Culture. VinUni is proud to be a
part of the Vingroup ecosystem, giving its students the opportunities to connect with several pioneering companies
(such as VinFast, Vinpearl, Vinmec, Vinschool, and Vinhomes) and a dynamic entrepreneurial ecosystem (such as
VinVenture, VinMotion, or VinRobotics…).
In September 2024, VinUni became the youngest and fastest university in the world to achieve QS 5 stars, earning 5 stars in 9
categories: global engagement, good governance, program strength, academic development, teaching, employability,
facilities, arts, and social impact. In October 2024, VinUni was appointed by UNESCO as the first and only UNESCO University
Chair, focusing on Environmental Leadership, Cultural Heritage, and Biodiversity. In March 2025, VinUni became the youngest
university in Vietnam to achieve institutional accreditation from the Foundation for International Business Administration
Accreditation (FIBAA) following international standards, only nine months after its founding cohort of students graduated and
within five years of its inception.
Since June 2025, VinUni has officially entered a new chapter in our development. We are gradually transitioning into a
research- and innovation-oriented university, with an ambitious vision to join the world’s top 100 universities. Our
strategic focus includes attracting global academic talent, establishing integrated research–innovation–industry
complexes, and launching excellent research clusters in priority areas. VinUni's development strategy is built on two
core pillars: enhancing academic and research infrastructure to international standards and strengthening academic
capacity to meet global benchmarks. To support this transformation, Vingroup - our benefactor - has committed an
additional investment of US$427 million into VinUni over the coming years.
VinUniversity proactively seeks to develop lasting collaborative relationships with leading universities and institutions
around the world for academic development activities. We cordially invite your esteemed university to explore the
opportunity to collaborate with us via the global exchange and short-term immersion programs in Vietnam with
VinUni.
We look forward to exchanging ideas and collaborating with you!

bachelor’s degrees or a bachelor’s and a master’s degree – one from VinUni and the other from a partner
university. What makes the integrated degree programs at VinUni stand out uniquely is the network of
prestigious partner universities that VinUni has built the program with and the scholarship opportunities
offered exclusively to VinUni students. The programs consist of two phases in which VinUni students will
study Phase 1 at VinUni and Phase 2 at a partner university overseas. Students joining the fast-tracking
Bachelor’s - Master’s module take classes towards their Master’s while earning their Bachelor’s degree,
helping to save significant costs of attendance and enabling them to graduate up to 12 months sooner. In
addition, accelerated degree pathways are intended to build on the momentum students currently have
during their undergraduate program. That is aimed to create a seamless progression toward higher
