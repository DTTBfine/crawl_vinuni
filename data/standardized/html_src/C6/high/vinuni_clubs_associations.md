## VINUNI ULTIMATE CLUB

VinUni Ultimate Club (VUC) comprises individuals who share a common interest in the sport of ultimate (a.k.a. ultimate Frisbee). What is unique about ultimate is a concept known as “spirit of the game,” which refers to fair play and sportsmanship. As a result, there is no referee in ultimate Frisbee. Our team values are Respect, Integrity, Teamwork, and Camaraderie. We aim to create an inclusive, friendly, and competitive environment where students excel in ultimate Frisbee while also expanding their social circles. Most importantly, we believe that ultimate is more than just a sport; it is a way to bridge social gaps and bring communities together.

* **Highlight:**

1. Schoolwide blood drive
2. Throwing clinic for local orphans
3. Home and away tournaments

* **For more information, please visit [our fanpage.](http://www.facebook.com/vinulti)**
