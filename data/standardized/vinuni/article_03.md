The College of Arts and Sciences cultivates six attributes for success: Character, Competence, Critical Thinking, Leadership, Entrepreneurship, and National Pride-Global Awareness. ## Academic Highlights
Our philosophy is built on a foundation of active learning, experiential learning, team-based learning and interdisciplinary research. We integrate technology-enhanced learning into our curriculum to enrich the educational experience, develop digital literacies and prepare students for the ever-evolving technological landscape. By embracing this multifaceted approach, VinUniversity students are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary to thrive in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society.

###  IGNITE Week 2025: A Celebration of Active Learning 
> 2025: The third annual Ignite Week: Celebration of Active Learning was held from April 14-18, 2025. This inspiring week-long celebration unites our dedicated community of educators and learners to advance the frontiers of active and experiential learning. Participants immersed themselves in Open Classroom Week, AI posters, Teaching Innovation Grants, and SparkPlug Presentations, exchanging pioneering teaching techniques and countless conversations about reimagining pedagogical practices. The event reinforced VinUniversity's mission to stay at the forefront of educational innovation and continue breaking new ground. 
[ See more ](https://www.youtube.com/embed/pvPU8vaRnNU?si=pqnoSGJFI1RbKZ8M)
###  Ignite Week 2024: A Celebration of Active Learning 
> The second annual IGNITE: Celebration of Active Learning Week took place from May 13-17, 2024. This annual event brings together the vibrant community of educators and learners at VinUniversity to celebrate the diversity of active learning, promote conversations about teaching and learning, and continually improve experiential learning at VinUniversity. This week-long event brings attention to active learning strategies, innovative teaching approaches, and the latest research on learning. Through this event, we aim to inspire new ideas and initiatives that will shape the future of education at VinUniversity and beyond. 
###  Teaching And Learning At VinUniversity 
> In these stories, VinUniversity faculty share their reflections on how they are creating an impactful, student-centered learning environment. They will share insights into how they have applied active learning to their courses, their use of feedback, collaboration, and other strategies that make our classrooms dynamic spaces for growth. Through their experiences, you'll see firsthand how our university cultivates a culture of pedagogical excellence. We value teaching as highly as research, putting students at the forefront of all we do.
