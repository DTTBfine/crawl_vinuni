#  Homepage
### **ABOUT US**
The University Registrar is a key, central and professional service department. The scope of services covers both undergraduate and postgraduate studies. Our mission is to maintain the integrity of the official record of student academic achievement for all colleges by providing academic planning services, data integrity services, and services to current and former students, faculty, staff, administrative staff, and external agencies.
We conserve students’ permanent academic record and provides services for students and faculty members at VinUniversity in the areas of academic policy, enrollment and course information. We act as VinUni’s proxy by coordinating activities which include, but are not limited to, course registration; assignment of classes to classrooms; maintenance of courses offered and grades awarded; certification of students for graduation; and maintenance, protection and release of academic records.
**VinUniversity – As A Member of AACRAO**
As one of the nation’s oldest and largest higher education associations, AACRAO _(American Association of Collegiate Registrars and Admissions Officers)_ is an expert and comprehensive resource for more than 11,000 higher education professionals in the U.S., Canada, and more than 40 other countries.
