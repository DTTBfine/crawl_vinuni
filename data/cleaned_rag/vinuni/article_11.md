### Forms & Petitions
This page provides quick access to commonly used forms related to academic procedures, course registration, and student records at VinUniversity. Please read the instructions carefully before submitting.
#### Official Documents

- Purpose: Transcript / Degree Certificate / Confirmation Letters / English Proficiency Certification Letter. Form: Student Document Request Form. Submission Method: Fill in online form using VinUni email.

#### Academic Requests

- Purpose: Course Registration (Retake / Audit / Individual Study). Form: N/A. Submission Method: Submit request via email to Registrar’s Office.
- Purpose: Course Withdrawal (After add/drop period). Form: N/A. Submission Method: Submit request via email with course instructor’s approval.
- Purpose: Credit Transfer. Form: VinUni Transfer Credit Form. Submission Method: Submit with supporting docs to registrar@vinuni.edu.vn.
- Purpose: Graduation Application. Form: N/A. Submission Method: Available online during graduation application period.
- Purpose: Double Degree. Form: Double Degree Form. Submission Method: Submit request via email to Registrar’s Office.

#### Program & Study Status Changes

- Purpose: Program Change (Major, Degree, or College). Form: VinUni Change of Program Major Minor. Submission Method: Submit to Program Director & Registrar.
- Purpose: Leave of Absence. Form: VinUni Defer Withdraw Form. Submission Method: Submit via email with advisor/PD’s approval.
- Purpose: Return to Study. Form: VinUni Request to return from a Leave of Absence. Submission Method: Submit at least 1 month before semester begins.

#### General petition
Students may use the General Petition form to submit academic or administrative requests that are not covered by existing standard forms. Common use cases include:
  * Requesting academic exceptions (e.g., credit overload, course changes after the deadline)
  * Requesting deadline extensions due to personal circumstances
  * Appealing the application of a policy in special cases

- Purpose: General Petition. Form: VinUni General Petition Form. Submission Method: Email to registrar@vinuni.edu.vn, CC your Academic Advisor and Program Director (if applicable).

#### Where to Get Help
  * Some forms may be available only upon request. If you’re unsure which form to use, contact the Registrar’s Office.
  * All forms must be submitted using your VinUni email address.
