#### How to Register for Courses
**Key Steps to Follow**
We recommend the following steps to ensure a smooth registration experience:
**1. Review Your Study Plan**  
Check your program’s suggested course roadmap (issued by your College).
**2. Consult Curriculum Framework**  
Understand your degree structure, course requirements, and prerequisites.  
 Curriculum Framework Link
**3. Plan Your Tentative Schedule**  
Use the planning period prior to registration opening to draft your preferred course list.
**4. Register on SIS**  
Log in to the Student Information System (SIS) and register for courses.
**5. Follow Add/Drop Timeline**  
During the first few days of the semester, you can make changes directly in SIS.
**6. Submit Special Requests**  
For retakes, audits, or withdrawals, contact the Registrar’s Office via email.

#### Course Registration Flow
#### How to Use SIS for Course Registration
Follow these key steps to register successfully on SIS (Student Information System):  
**1. Log in to SIS**  
 Go to SIS  
Use your VinUni email and password.
**2. Go to Registration portal**  
Go to “Academics” → “Course Registration” → see the correct term and click “Register” if the status is “Open”.
**3. Search for Courses**  
Use course code or name to search. Filter by College, semester part (Full / A / B), or time slot.
**4. Add Courses to Cart and Register**  
Click “Add,” then “Register” to finalize. Be careful to check the status of the course is “Registered” (not “Selected”; if it is “Selected”, it means that the course has not been registered).
**5. Confirm Registration**  
Check your registered courses under “Your Class Schedule.”

#### Additional Notes
**1. Course Capacity & Waitlist**
  * Some courses have limited seats; once full, students will not be able to register unless a spot opens.
  * No waitlist function is currently available in SIS. Students are advised to check SIS regularly for any open slots.

**2. Prerequisite Enforcement**
  * SIS automatically blocks registration if prerequisites are not met.
  * If you believe you are eligible but cannot register, contact Registrar’s Office.

**3. Cohort-specific Restrictions**
  * Certain courses are restricted to specific cohorts.
  * Check the course notes or announcements from your College.

**4. Time Conflicts**
  * SIS will not allow you to register for overlapping courses.
  * If needed, consult with Academic Advisor for alternative options.
